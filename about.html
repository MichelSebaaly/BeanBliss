Our Company

Our History

Bean Bliss has a rich history steeped in the love and art of coffee. It all started over a decade ago when our founder, Michel Sebaaly , took the leap of faith to pursue a dream—a dream of creating a coffee haven where every sip would transport people to a world of bliss.

From humble beginnings as a small coffee cart in a local farmers market, Bean Bliss quickly gained a loyal following.

The exceptional quality of our beans, carefully sourced from sustainable farms around the world, and the meticulous craftsmanship of our baristas set us apart.

Driven by a relentless passion for perfection, Bean Bliss expanded into a charming brick-and-mortar coffee shop. Word spread like wildfire, and soon, coffee enthusiasts flocked to experience the blissful aroma and taste that became synonymous with our name.

Over the years, Bean Bliss has become an integral part of the community—a gathering place where friends reunite, new friendships are forged, and coffee lovers find solace. We take pride in nurturing these connections and providing an atmosphere that exudes warmth and comfort.

From our early days to the present, Bean Bliss remains committed to our core values of quality, sustainability, and the joy of shared coffee experiences. We continue to explore new flavors, techniques, and brewing methods to bring innovation and excitement to every cup.

As we look ahead, our journey is fueled by the love and support of our loyal customers, who have become our extended family. Together, we'll continue to elevate the coffee experience, one blissful cup at a time.

Thank you for being part of our Bean Bliss story, and we invite you to join us on this journey of indulgence, connection, and pure coffee bliss.

coffee & Craft

It takes many hands to craft the perfect cup of coffee-from the farmers who tend to the red-ripe coffee cherries, to the master roasters who coax the best from every bean, and to the barista who serves it with care. We are committed to the highest standards of quality and service, embracing our heritage while innovating to create new experiences to savor.

Our Partners

We like to say that we are not in the coffee business serving people, but in the people business serving coffee. Our employees-who we call partners-are at the heart of the Bean Bliss experience. We are committed to making our partners proud and investing in their health, well-being and success and to creating a culture of belonging where everyone is welcome.
